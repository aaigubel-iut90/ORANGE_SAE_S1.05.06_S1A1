Orange through Numbers


Welcome to the economy page.

This page aims to describe the economy of Orange.
Orange's revenue in 2023 was €43.47 billion.
Orange's revenue is on average higher than other companies in the industry,
however, Orange's revenue has continually decreased between 2014 and 2020, falling from 23,763 in 2014 to 22,603 in 2020,
representing a decrease of approximately 4.9 percentage points.
Although Orange has a significant share of the French and more generally European telecommunications market,
it is not the leading company in terms of the number of subscribers globally, as indicated by this ranking.
